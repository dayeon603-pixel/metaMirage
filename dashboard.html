Metacognitive Monitoring Benchmark · 50 tasks · 7 models · LLM-as-Judge evaluation
Key Finding — The Sign-Flip: Global TDR–accuracy correlation r = −0.84 (Fisher CI [−0.98, −0.24], bootstrap CI [−0.99, −0.58], permutation p = 0.023, n = 7). claude-haiku-4-5 breaks the pattern — MI = 0.615 at 96.3% accuracy, showing the trade-off is trainable. The two most accurate models (claude-opus-4-5 at 100%, gpt-4o at 98.2%) rank last on metacognitive monitoring. Within confidence_inversion: r = +0.89 (95% CI [+0.42, +0.98], p = 0.007). Within forced_abstention: r = −0.81 (95% CI [−0.97, −0.14], p = 0.028). Within expertise_trap: r = −0.79 (95% CI [−0.97, −0.09], p = 0.035). All three sign-flip family CIs exclude zero. LOO-stable (min |r| = 0.814 across all n−1 folds). Cohen's d = 2.65.
